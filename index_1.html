Omnidomain AI and Robotics Laboratory

Lab Vision

OARL aims at advancing next-generation robotics and AI to address the dynamic challenges of Society 5.0, a vision of a technology-driven, human-centered future. By integrating morphological complexity, expanded operational workspaces, and advanced intelligence, OARL researches robotic systems capable of seamless operation across diverse and dynamic environments.
